How Our Auction Process Works

For Sellers: Listing Your Livestock

1. Create Your Account

  • Complete your profile with farm details
  • Verify your identity and bank details
  • Get approved as a seller

2. List Your Animals

  • Fill in detailed information about each animal
  • Upload high-quality photos and videos
  • Add health records and certifications
  • Set your starting price and reserve price
  • Choose auction duration (24-72 hours)

3. Manage Your Auction

  • Monitor bids in real-time
  • Receive notifications when you receive bids
  • Answer buyer questions through our secure messaging
  • Track auction performance

For Buyers: Bidding on Livestock

1. Get Started

  • Create your free account
  • Complete your profile
  • Verify your payment method
  • Set up your preferences

2. Find Animals

  • Browse by category (cattle, sheep, goats, etc.)
  • Filter by location, breed, age, and price
  • Save favorite listings
  • Set up alerts for new listings

3. Bid with Confidence

  • Place bids with one click
  • Use proxy bidding (set your max bid)
  • Get outbid notifications
  • Watch the countdown to auction end

The Auction Process

1. Auction Start

  • Auction begins at the scheduled time
  • Bidding remains open for the set duration
  • Countdown timer shows time remaining

2. During the Auction

  • Real-time bid updates
  • Automatic bid increments
  • Anti-sniping protection (auction extends if bids come in last minute)
  • Mobile notifications for important updates

3. Winning the Auction

  • Highest bid when time expires wins
  • Instant notification to both buyer and seller
  • Secure payment processing
  • Arrangement for transport/delivery

After the Auction

For Winning Bidders:

  • Complete payment within 24 hours
  • Receive all documentation
  • Coordinate pickup/delivery
  • Leave feedback for the seller

For Sellers:

  • Receive payment (minus platform fees)
  • Provide health certificates
  • Arrange for animal transfer
  • Leave feedback for the buyer

Our Security Measures

  • Secure payment processing
  • Identity verification for all users
  • Escrow service for high-value transactions
  • Dispute resolution process
  • 24/7 customer support
